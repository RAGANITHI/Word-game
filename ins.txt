1.ADA will choose a four letter word.
2.You have to guess that word by giving random words.
3.If each letter match with the computer word at exact position
	then, it displays "(no of correct letters) CORRECT"
	eg: 3 C-3 letters are correct and are correct position.
4.If the letters are correct but placed in a wrong position 
	then, it displays "(no of correct letters) DISPLACED"
	eg: 2 D-letters are correct but placed displaced.
5.words containing repetitive letters are not used (eg.loop,dead,pool etc)
6.Person with minimum trials will the winner.
